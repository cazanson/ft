The Summa Theologiae of St Thomas Aquinas
in the Corpus Thomisticum edition
by R. Busa SJ and E. Alarcón
is available in an App for Android devices.

The App is called STh It.
This application allows users to:

read,
search the Summa by keywords,
by parts,
by customizable bookmarks,
by treatises
and by indexes
all in Latin.

Each item of the Summa has its own identification number
- index of the Corpus Thomisticum Project.

Users can navigate the menus in five different languages:
English, Spanish, French, Polish and Italian.

The aim of the application is to provide students and scholars with
a tool for basic research on Thomas Aquinas,
that could also be useful during lectures, seminars etc.

The App was designed and developed by:
Andrzej Nakonieczny OP,
a son of the Province of Poland of the Order of Preachers,
with cooperation of the staff
of The Thomistic Institute in Warsaw:
Michał Mrozek OP, Michał Golubiewski OP,
Paweł Krupa OP and Zbigniew Pajda OP.

Developer owes Thérèse Bonin the corrected version
of the presentation of the STh lt.

The App is available for free download
and once downloaded it is available offline.

Aquinas's Opera omnia
in the Corpus Thomisticum edition
by Enrique Alarcón,
are available in an App for Android devices.

The App is called CTh It.
Basic research tool for works of St. Thomas Aquinas.

This application allows users to:

read works of St. Thomas Aquinas,
search them by keywords
by customizable bookmarks,
and by indexes
all in Latin.

The text is taken from the website
of the Corpus Thomisticum Project.

Users can navigate the menus in five different languages:
English, Spanish, French, Polish and Italian.

The aim of the application is to provide students and scholars with
a tool for basic research on Thomas Aquinas, available offline
(e. g., during seminars or lectures).

The App was designed and developed by:
Andrzej Nakonieczny OP,
a son of the Province of Poland of the Order of Preachers,
with cooperation of the staff
of The Thomistic Institute in Warsaw:
Mateusz Przanowski OP, Michał Mrozek OP, Michał Golubiewski OP,
Paweł Krupa OP and Zbigniew Pajda OP.

The App is available for free download
and once downloaded it is available offline.
